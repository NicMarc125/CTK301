Everything Is Falling

This album is lowkey my magnum opus! It is most definitly my best produced one and it puts everyone who was involved talents on display! I played all the instruments on this one and my friend Abby sang. We were in a group together previously called Bloom Kings, and the reason we switched over was because none of us were feeling BK no more. (Plus Abby is a better singer than me) This consistes of 9 tracks all telling a story of heartbreak and f-ed up friendships, and I like how the album gets heavier as you go along!

See You Space Cat

This is the first time Abby sand in a Bloom Kings Track! The idea behind this song was to flippin' turn EVERYTHING up to 100! I am a big fan of complicted riffs and structures and that's exactly what I was going for in this song, I like pushing myself and those around me to their creative limits, because I have a firm belief that every has a pool of untapped potential and sometimes you just gotta give yourself a little nudge. This is what that nudge lead to!

Autumn Leaves

This was the first song I made when I arrived at ISU in the fall! I wanted a more emotional feel for my music and tread the waters of genres I haven't been in before to expand my taste and skill. This song was also the last song I made before I switched DAWS. The raw emotional sound of Autumn Leaves is something I still resonate with today and the whole reason I make music. To provide comfort to those who need it. I mean that's why I listen to music!
