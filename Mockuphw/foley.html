The Story

4 low-class members of this futuristic dystopian society, who would do anything to get a buck, all happen to get approached by a formally suited man and get handed a black card with green text. The card has a number. The number recruits people to send to an abandoned miracle research space station. It is told that that station has data and research leading to many cures. Something has happened to the ship though, and communication has been lost.
